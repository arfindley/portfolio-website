SplashSync

Overview

SplashSync is a responsive web app that simplifies real-time wind, wave, and weather reports for sailors, surfers, and divers.

Designed for safety and ease, it transforms complex data into clear, visual insights, helping users make informed decisions.

With personalized onboarding, intuitive navigation, and safety recommendations, SplashSync ensures water sports enthusiasts

can enjoy stress-free, well-prepared days on the water.
